## SCENE BREAKDOWN

- **Wall**: Warm cream background for cozy feel
- **Hearth**: Layered stone in warm grays/browns for depth and stone texture
- **Firebox**: Dark shadow cavity where fire lives
- **Logs**: Two dark wooden logs stacked for realistic fire base
- **Embers**: Three glowing circles (orange, red, yellow) suggesting active flames and heat

Paper cut-out style ✅: Flat solids only, distinct layered depth planes, no gradients or filters.
