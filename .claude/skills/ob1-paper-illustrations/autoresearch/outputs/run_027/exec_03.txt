## Design Notes

✅ **Paper cut-out style:** 9 flat layers, zero gradients (the Milky Way is a solid-colored stroke, not a gradient)
✅ **Night mood:** Deep navy sky (#0F1B3C) with progressively lighter hill silhouettes
✅ **Full moon:** Warm off-white (#F5E6D3) positioned high in the composition
✅ **Milky Way:** Soft cream-colored arc suggesting the galactic band
✅ **Stars:** Three crisp white points scattered across the sky
✅ **Complete & compilable:** All 9 elements fully written, all tags closed, no truncation
