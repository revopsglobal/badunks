# MealsIllustration — Kitchen Scene

I'll create a cozy kitchen scene with a cast iron pot, wooden cutting board, and fresh herbs using the paper cut-out style.

**PLAN:**
- LAYERS: cream wall | shelf shadow | wooden shelf | pot + board + herbs (foreground) | accent shadows
- ELEMENT BUDGET: ~6 per layer = ~28 total
- Subject priority: cast iron pot, cutting board, herb sprigs appear immediately after palette
